EBS volumes can only be mounted to one instance at a time.
An instance can have multiple EBS volumes mounted to it.

EBS volumes can only be used in the AZ where they were created.
They are replicated in the same AZ but they are not HA.

EBS Volumes
  HDD
	ThroughPut Optimized (st1)
	Not a boot device
	Ideal for frequent access and throughput intense work
	Max throughput = 500 MiB/s

	Cold HDD (sc1)
	Not a boot device
	Ideal for infrequent access and lowest storage cost
